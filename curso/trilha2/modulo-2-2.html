💡 Como ler este módulo

Cada preset tem 4 partes: string canônica (copie literal) · gatilhos (quando usar) · exemplo completo (template já montado) · variações permitidas vs proibidas (o que você pode mudar).

Os exemplos usam o template [0s][3s][6s] do módulo 2.1. Se você ainda não leu aquele módulo, volte antes de continuar.

1

🏜️ DUNE EPIC

O preset mais rígido. 13 em 16 amostras retornaram a string exatamente igual nos testes. É o "estado sólido" da taxonomia — use copiando literal, não reformule.

📝 String canônica (copiar literal)

color_system: "burnt amber+pure black only, hard directional low sun, human tiny vs world"
camera_style: "aerial pull-back / extreme low-angle / overhead top-down"
techniques: ["cloth inertia", "sand displacement", "120fps slow-motion", "hard directional lighting"]

🎯 Gatilhos de ativação

Paisagens épicas · escala · desertos · espaço · vast nature · figuras míticas · lone individual against world · qualquer coisa que demanda grandeur.

📄 Exemplo completo — monge em montanha

same character throughout all shots [0s] Aerial pull-back revealing a lone monk in red robes walking across an endless plateau of cracked black basalt, cloth inertia trailing behind in wind, burnt amber+pure black only, hard directional low sun casting impossibly long shadow. Phase 1 audio: practical sounds only — distant wind, gravel underfoot, fabric flapping. [3s] Extreme low-angle tracking shot up to his silhouette against the sun, 120fps slow-motion captures a single dust particle floating past his face, burnt amber deepens to bronze. Phase 2 audio: single low brass chord arrives, building tension. [6s] Overhead top-down shot revealing the vastness of the plateau beneath him. STOP MOTION 3s — complete audio silence — monk frozen mid-stride, robes suspended in wind, a single grain of sand floating in mid-air — explosive snap-back to 24fps as wind roars back and he continues walking. Phase 3 audio: full orchestral score crescendo with 3x audio density. Anamorphic 2.39:1.

✓ Variações permitidas

  • • Trocar "sand" por "snow" ou "salt" em color/physics
  • • Adicionar "cloak", "armor", "spear" ao sujeito
  • • Trocar "sun" por "moon" (mudando também "low sun" → "cold moonlight")
  • • Adaptar para cenário de gelo, espaço, oceano

✗ Variações proibidas

  • • Adicionar cores à paleta (quebra o "amber+black only")
  • • Usar close-up no [0s] (vai contra "tiny vs world")
  • • Cena urbana ou interior
  • • Mais de um personagem principal
2

🥊 JOHN WICK ACTION

Override hard-coded para combate. Regra explícita: "Combat ALWAYS routes to JOHN WICK ACTION, even if the setting suggests another preset". Kung fu em bambuzal → JOHN WICK com paleta verde. Duelos no deserto → JOHN WICK com paleta âmbar. O vocabulário passa pelo filtro de moderação em situações que outros não passam.

📝 String canônica

color_system: "hyper-saturated blue+red+black, wet neon surfaces, anamorphic 2.39:1"
camera_style: "extreme low-angle tracking / crash zoom / handheld natural lag"
techniques: ["cloth inertia physics", "skin distorting on impact", "120fps slow-motion snap-back", "stop-motion peak tension"]

🎯 Gatilhos

Combate · luta · perseguição · violência cinética · armas · cenários táticos · movimento urbano intenso. Também: qualquer coisa que envolva duas pessoas trocando golpes ou tiros.

📄 Exemplo completo — guerreira no metrô

same character throughout all shots [0s] Extreme low-angle tracking shot of a female warrior in red kimono sprinting down a flooded Tokyo metro corridor, cloth inertia physics as fabric trails, hyper-saturated blue+red+black, wet neon surfaces reflecting her silhouette. Phase 1 audio: practical sounds only — sprinkler water, metal footsteps, distant train rumble. [3s] Crash zoom into her face as she pivots, 120fps slow-motion captures water droplets mid-air around her spinning kimono, neon red intensifying. Phase 2 audio: single low brass chord arrives, building tension. [6s] Handheld natural lag shot of the decisive strike. STOP MOTION 2s — complete audio silence — her fist frozen an inch from the opponent's chest, water droplets suspended around the impact zone, a single bead of sweat mid-fall — explosive snap-back to 24fps as impact erupts and sends both fighters sliding. Phase 3 audio: full orchestral score crescendo with 3x audio density. Anamorphic 2.39:1.

💡 Adaptação de paleta para combat em outros cenários

  • • Kung fu em bambuzal: "hyper-saturated emerald green bamboo + deep shadow black, wet morning dew surfaces"
  • • Duelo no deserto: "hyper-saturated burnt amber+dust-orange+black, dry scorched surfaces"
  • • Luta espacial: "hyper-saturated cyan+magenta+void-black, zero-g reflection surfaces"

Regra: mantenha "hyper-saturated" + 3 cores + "anamorphic 2.39:1". Troque só as cores específicas.

3

🌃 BLADE RUNNER NOIR

O único preset onde a paleta adapta livremente. É mood noir moderno, não uma paleta literal. Para cenas de velas use âmbar, para retro use chrome+vermelho, para bar use verde — sempre preservando "anamorphic 2.39:1" e o mood íntimo urbano.

📝 String canônica

color_system (padrão): "hyper-saturated blue+red+black, wet neon surfaces, anamorphic 2.39:1"
ADAPT freely — este preset é mood, não paleta literal
camera_style: "slow dolly-in / extreme low-angle / handheld natural lag"
techniques: ["cloth inertia", "120fps slow-motion", "floor puddle mirror reflection", "anamorphic lens flares"]

🎯 Gatilhos

Urban night · cenas íntimas não-ação · cyberpunk · interiores com neon · qualquer coisa moody indoor/urban. Ideal para momentos contemplativos, solidão, drama pessoal.

Phase 1 audio: practical sounds only — ice clinking, distant piano, someone laughing in the next room. [3s] Extreme low-angle on his hands wrapping around the glass, 120fps slow-motion captures a drop of whiskey rolling down the rim, amber intensifies to honey. Phase 2 audio: single low brass chord arrives, building tension. [6s] Handheld natural lag as he looks up, catching the reflection of someone watching him in the bar mirror. STOP MOTION 2s — complete audio silence — his eye frozen mid-glance, reflection locked, single candle flame suspended — explosive snap-back to 24fps as he slowly sets the glass down. Phase 3 audio: full orchestral score crescendo with 3x audio density. Anamorphic 2.39:1.

✓ Exemplos de paleta adaptada

  • • Candlelight: "warm amber + deep black shadow, candlelight flicker physics"
  • • Retro diner: "hyper-saturated chrome+red+cream, 50s neon signage"

⛈️ STORM EPIC

Preset onde o clima é o protagonista. O micro-hack empírico do autor: especificar "lightning only light source 0.7s each flash" — a duração literal do flash melhora o render consistentemente.

📝 String canônica

color_system: "dark overcast storm clouds, lightning only light source 0.7s each flash, deep ocean blue-black water"
camera_style: "aerial pull-back / extreme low-angle / overhead top-down"
techniques: ["lightning flash illumination", "water surface tension physics", "120fps slow-motion snap-back", "STOP MOTION at peak impact"]

🎯 Gatilhos

Storms · lightning · tornados · tsunamis · furacões · violent weather. O personagem é secundário — a força natural domina o enquadramento.

📄 Exemplo completo — pescador contra onda gigante

Phase 1 audio: practical sounds only — wind howling, ropes creaking, distant thunder. [3s] Extreme low-angle from the boat deck looking up as the wave towers overhead, 120fps slow-motion captures a single white crest of foam curling, lightning strobes. Phase 2 audio: single low brass chord arrives, building tension. [6s] Overhead top-down of the moment of impact. STOP MOTION 3s — complete audio silence — wave frozen at peak height, fisherman suspended mid-brace on the deck, a single droplet of water held mid-air against the lightning flash — explosive snap-back to 24fps as the wall collapses and water engulfs the boat. Phase 3 audio: full orchestral score crescendo with 3x audio density. Anamorphic 2.39:1.

⚡ ANIME SHONEN EPIC

Vocabulário autoral de anime. "Electric blue core aura" é o termo que gera o glow característico. "Electrical particle physics" é o que faz os raios crepitarem. Sem esses termos exatos, o modelo entrega anime genérico.

📝 String canônica

color_system: "electric blue core aura + golden lightning crackling + pure white energy bursts against deep black void"
camera_style: "extreme low-angle / 360° orbit / crash zoom"
techniques: ["cloth inertia on uniform", "electrical particle physics", "120fps slow-motion energy buildup", "anamorphic lens flares"]

🎯 Gatilhos

Lutas de anime · energy auras · transformações · power-ups · shonen tropes · Dragon Ball · Naruto · Demon Slayer · Jujutsu Kaisen · Bleach.

📄 Exemplo completo — transformação shonen

[3s] 360° orbit around him as he screams and the aura explodes upward, 120fps slow-motion energy buildup captures individual lightning sparks arcing off his body, golden lightning crackling around blue core. Phase 2 audio: single low brass chord arrives, building tension. [6s] Crash zoom onto his face as he opens his eyes, now glowing white. STOP MOTION 4s — complete audio silence — warrior frozen mid-transformation, aura suspended like a halo of blue fire around him, individual golden lightning bolts held mid-arc, a single tear of pure white energy rising from his cheek — explosive snap-back to 24fps as the aura erupts and shatters the ground around him.
